The best movies/shows according to Athena

My top 3 favourites of all-time

Never have I ever

This show revolves around an Indian girl Devi, settled in the US. It explores her journey through college and how she handles the death of someone really close to her.

The bold type

This series has 6 seasons. It is based in New York, and explores the journey of 3 women- their careers, relationships and friendship.

Lion

This academy award nominee is about a child who was accidentally separated from his family, how he find's a home thousands of miles away, and how he attempts to reunite with his birth family years later.
