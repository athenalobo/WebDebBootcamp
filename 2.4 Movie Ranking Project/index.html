The best movies/shows according to Athena

My top 3 favourites of all-time

Never have I ever

This show is a teen rom-com. It is about an Indian girl, settled in the US. She is a rebel and quite dramatic at first, but the show explains her journey through college and how she handles the death of someone really close to her.

The bold type

This series has 6 seasons. It is based in New York, and explains the journey of 3 empowered women, as they navigate their careers, love life and friendship.

Mia culpa

This is a spanish romantic drama, and it is easily my favourite movie of all time. It is an adaptation of a trilogy, and i can't wait for the next 2 movies. It is based on the intense, forbidden love story between Noah and Nick, both coming from broken homes. They meet each other when Noah's mom and Nick's dad get married, making their love incestuous and hence forbidden.
